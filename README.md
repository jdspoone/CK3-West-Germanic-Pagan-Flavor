# West Germanic Pagan Flavor

A simple and lightweight mod attempting to add a bit more flavour for West Germanic Pagans.

## Features:

### West Germanic Conversion Decision:
Enables West Germanic Christian rulers during the Tribal Era, who are Duke-Tier rulers, or Independent Count-tier rulers, and whose primary title is within England or Scotland, to convert via decision to Asatru. The historical record would indicate that Anglo-Saxon lords would intermittently renounce Christianity and re-embrace the Germanic faith well after the supposed wholesale adoption of Christianity. This mod attempts to model this in game. It should have the effect of creating a more unstable and dynamic political landscape in England and the lowlands of Scotland during the 867 start, as Anglo-Saxon duke and independent counts will occasionally abandon Christianity in favour of Asatru, potentially then allying themselves with the Norse invaders against their erstwhile co-religionists.

Additional features planned!

## Compatibility:
This mod does not override any base-game functionality, and should be compatible with any other mod.
