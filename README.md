# West Germanic Pagan Flavor

A simple and relatively lightweight mod attempting to add a bit more flavour for West Germanic (i.e. Anglo-Saxon) pagans.

## Current Features

### New West Germanic Pagan Faith
Inspired by [The Fallen Eagle](https://steamcommunity.com/workshop/filedetails/?id=2243307127), this mod adds a new West Germanic pagan faith, **Eseism**, within the broader Germanic Religion. Holy sites are currently limited within England to fit the focus of the mod, and to enable usage of Christian restricted Special Buildings implemented by the incredible [Medieval Arts](https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2452585382). As such, bonuses for ownership the new Eseist holy sites have been reduced below the level of base-game holy sites for the purposes of balance.

### West Germanic Conversion Decision
Enables West Germanic Christian rulers whose primary title is within England or Scotland, to convert via decision to Eseism during the Tribal Era. To assist with realm stability (primarily for underwhelming AI rulers), non-zealous courtiers and vassals will convert with their liege, and the capital county will receive a positive modifier and it's faith will convert as well. AI Rulers are required to be a Duke or an Independent Count, while no such restrictions apply to Player Characters. AI rulers have a chance of taking this decision depending on their personality traits. 

The historical record would indicate that Anglo-Saxon lords would intermittently renounce Christianity and re-embrace the Germanic faith well after the supposed wholesale adoption of Christianity. This mod attempts to represent this in game. It should have the effect of creating a more unstable and dynamic political landscape in England and the lowlands of Scotland during the 867 start, as Anglo-Saxon rulers will occasionally abandon Christianity in favour of Eseism, potentially then allying themselves with the Norse Asatru invaders against their erstwhile co-religionists.

## Dependencies and Recommendations
This mod has no dependencies, however I recommend using it alongside the following:

- [Medieval Arts](https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2452585382)

- [Anglo Saxon Flavorization](https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3630785964)

## Compatibility
This mod overrides the base-game definition of *germanic_religion*, defined in *common/religion/religions/00_germanic.txt*. Any other mods overriding the same definition will be incompatible.
