A simple and lightweight mod attempting to add a bit more flavour for West Germanic Pagans.

Features:
1. Enables West Germanic Christian rulers during the Tribal Era, whose primary title is of Duchy-tier rank and is within England or Scotland, to convert via decision to Asatru. The historical record seems to indicate that Anglo-Saxon lords would intermittently renounce Christianity and re-embrace the Germanic faith well after the supposed wholesale adoption of Christianity. This mod attempts to model that in game. It should have the effect of creating a more unstable and dynamic political landscape in England and the lowlands of Scotland during the 867 start, as Anglo-Saxon dukes will occasionally abandon Christianity in favour of Asatru, potentially then allying themselves with the Norse invaders against their erstwhile co-religionists.

Compatibility:
This mod does not override any base-game functionality, and should be compatible with any other mod.
