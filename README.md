# West Germanic Pagan Flavor

A simple and lightweight mod attempting to add a bit more flavour for West Germanic Pagans.

## Current Features:

### West Germanic Conversion Decision:
Enables West Germanic Christian rulers whose primary title is within England or Scotland, to convert via decision to Asatru during the Tribal Era. To assist with realm stability (primarily for underwhelming AI rulers), non-zealous courtiers and vassals will convert with their liege, and the capital county will receive a positive modifier and it's faith will convert as well. AI Rulers are required to be a Duke or an Independent Count, while no such restrictions apply to Player Characters. AI rulers have a chance of taking this decision depending on their personality traits. 

The historical record would indicate that Anglo-Saxon lords would intermittently renounce Christianity and re-embrace the Germanic faith well after the supposed wholesale adoption of Christianity. This mod attempts to represent this in game. It should have the effect of creating a more unstable and dynamic political landscape in England and the lowlands of Scotland during the 867 start, as Anglo-Saxon rulers will occasionally abandon Christianity in favour of Asatru, potentially then allying themselves with the Norse invaders against their erstwhile co-religionists.

This mod does not currently override any base-game functionality, and should be compatible with any other mods, excluding total-conversion mods.
